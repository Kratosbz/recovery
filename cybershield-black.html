Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your recovery.

How much does it cost to start a case?

The initial assessment is completely free. We only charge a success fee upon confirmed recovery — you pay nothing unless we recover your funds.

How long does recovery take?

Simple bank transfers: 2 to 4 weeks. Cryptocurrency and international cases: 4 to 12 weeks. You receive regular updates throughout.

Is my information safe?

All data is AES-256 encrypted. We are ISO 27001 certified and NDPR compliant. Your data is never shared without your consent.

Do you work with law enforcement?

Yes. We are certified EFCC partners and collaborate with the Nigeria Police Cybercrime Unit, INTERPOL, and international financial intelligence units.

What if the scammer is abroad?

International cases are our specialty. We have relationships with intelligence units in over 35 countries enabling effective cross-border recovery.
