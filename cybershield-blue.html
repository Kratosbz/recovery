Investment and Forex Fraud

Recovery from fake trading platforms, unregulated brokers, and Ponzi schemes. We work with international financial regulators to freeze and repatriate funds.

Romance and Social Media Scams

Identification, tracking, and prosecution of romance scammers. We trace money mule networks and coordinate with law enforcement for fund seizure.

Bank Transfer Fraud (BEC)

Mobile Money and POS Fraud

Recovery of funds lost via unauthorized mobile transfers, fake POS terminals, SIM swap fraud, and compromised mobile banking applications.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your recovery.

How much does it cost to start a case?

The initial case review and assessment is completely free. We only charge a success fee upon confirmed recovery, meaning you pay nothing unless we recover your funds. Our fee structure is disclosed upfront with full transparency.

How long does the recovery process take?

Timelines vary by case complexity. Simple bank transfers may be resolved in 2 to 4 weeks. Cryptocurrency and international fraud cases typically take 4 to 12 weeks. We provide regular progress updates throughout the process.

What types of fraud can you recover from?

We handle cryptocurrency fraud, investment scams, romance scams, business email compromise (BEC), ransomware, forex fraud, and mobile money theft. If you are unsure, submit your case and our analysts will evaluate it for free.

Is my personal information safe with you?

All information is encrypted using AES-256 standards under strict confidentiality protocols. We are ISO 27001 certified and compliant with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR). Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent.

Do you work with Nigerian law enforcement?

Yes. We are certified partners with the EFCC and collaborate with the Nigeria Police Force Cybercrime Unit, INTERPOL, and international financial intelligence units to pursue recovery through proper legal channels.

What if the scammer is based abroad?

International cases are our specialty. We have established relationships with financial intelligence units and law enforcement agencies in over 35 countries, enabling us to pursue recovery across borders effectively.
